李祥春 教授 博士生导师

Xiangchun Li is a professor in Tianjin Medical University Cancer Institute and Hospital. He obtained his PhD from CUHK in 2016. As of Dec 2018, he has published more than 20 peer-reviewed articles in international journals, including Nature, Lancet Oncology, JAMA Oncology, Gut and PNAS etc. Prof. Li’s research focuses on characterising mutational landscape of human gastrointestinal cancer, elucidating the association between mutational footprints and environmental factors, identifying biomarkers with translational significance in immunotherapy and early cancer diagnosis, and interpreting medical imaging data using deep learning algorithms.
